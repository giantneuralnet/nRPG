DROP RPG

Swords, shields, potions, bombs, elites, ice, zombies, clouds, and curses.

N = number of choices: 5
Difficulty

INFO

Items

Sword: gain attack. Shield: gain defense. Potion: heal. Poison: add poison to attacks.

Power potion: temporary attack or defense. Regen potion: periodic healing. Vampire potion: life steal. Chest: random reward.

Bombs: normal, clear, random, weaken, strength, cloud, poison, heal, lightning, ice, zombie.

Scrolls: stone makes one monster permanent stone. curse makes monsters rise once as ghosts.

Kill random can kill a monster or you. Heal random fully heals one target. Flash bang blinds everyone for 5 seconds.

Exile: queues current monsters for later spawns. Health swap: shuffles HP across the hero and monsters.

Monsters

Elite: stronger and worth more XP. Shielded: first direct attack breaks shield and deals no damage.

Zombie: fights other monsters and counters you. Ghost: resurrected haunted monster. Haunted: rises as ghost when killed.

Stone: cannot move or take damage. Frozen: temporarily cannot counter. Poisoned: takes periodic damage.

Doors move between rooms 1-3. Each room remembers what was left inside.
